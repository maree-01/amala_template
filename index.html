The Amala

A Tranquil Sanctuary in the Heart of Seminyak

The Amala is an urban oasis, with lush intimate villas and a unique wellness retreat in the heart of Seminyak. At The Amala, the Zen-inspired design fosters the best of two worlds – a secluded and luxurious place surrounded in harmony with nature and easy access to the vibrancy of nearby beaches, shops, restaurants and nightlife.

Stroll down verdant-shaded bamboo paths, dine on healthy cuisine, take a meditative yoga class in the Wellness Studio and luxuriate in a comprehensive choice of blissful Spa treatments. We look forward to welcoming you.
